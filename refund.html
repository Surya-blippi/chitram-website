Pricing, Cancellations and Refunds Policy

Subscription Plans

Chitram offers premium plans to access enhanced video creation features on the app. Users can choose between two subscription options:

  • Monthly Plan (30 days validity)
  • Annual Plan (365 days validity)

Subscription Terms and Payment

Recurring Subscription Fee

  • You can access premium features by agreeing to pay the subscription fee for your chosen plan
  • The subscription automatically renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal up to 96 hours prior to the end of the current period

UPI Autopay

  • Our subscription transactions in India use UPI autopay to setup and manage subscription payments
  • UPI auto-pay enables automatic renewal of your subscription before expiry, ensuring uninterrupted service
  • Renewal charges are processed up to 96 hours before plan expiry to ensure continuous access to premium features
  • If a subscription payment fails, we may retry charging your account for up to 20 days after the subscription expiry date
  • After 20 days of failed payment attempts, your subscription will be automatically canceled

Payment Options

If you prefer not to use auto-pay, you can:

  • Uncheck the autopay option during payment
  • Choose an alternative payment method available for your account

You can manage your subscription status through your account settings at any time

Cancellation Policy

  1. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings
  2. Upon cancellation:
    • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
    • You will continue to receive premium benefits until the last day of your paid period
    • No partial refunds will be provided for unused periods
  3. To request cancellation, you may also email us at chitramapp@gmail.com with your registered email address

Refund Policy

  1. All payments made are non-refundable
  2. We do not provide partial refunds for:
    • Unused subscription periods
    • Cancellation of subscription before the end of billing period
    • Change of mind or accidental purchases
  3. Exceptions may be made in cases of:
    • Technical errors in service delivery
    • Unauthorized transactions (with proper documentation)
  4. Refund requests, if any, must be submitted to chitramapp@gmail.com with:
    • Your registered email address
    • Transaction details
    • Reason for refund request

General Terms

  1. The company reserves the right to modify subscription plans and pricing at any time
  2. Existing subscribers will be notified of any price changes before their next billing cycle
  3. In case of multiple interpretations of any terms mentioned above, the company reserves the right to make the final and binding interpretation
  4. All disputes will be subject to the laws of the Republic of India and the courts of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh shall have exclusive jurisdiction

For any queries regarding pricing, cancellations, or refunds, please contact us at chitramapp@gmail.com
